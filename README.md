Project Overview
Interactive Excel dashboard for analyzing product sales, profits, orders, and regional performance. Includes slicers, KPIs, charts, and detailed insights for 2023–24 sales data.

Objectives
•	Analyze overall business performance using key KPIs.
•	Identify top-performing products, cities, and categories.
•	Understand trends across months, years, and regions.
•	Provide an interactive dashboard for quick decision-making.

Problem Statement
The raw sales data was difficult to interpret, making it challenging for management to identify trends and performance insights. A centralized, visual dashboard was required to understand sales, profit, and product performance effectively.

Solution
An interactive Excel dashboard was developed using Pivot Tables, Charts, and Slicers to visualize KPIs, trends, and product performance. The dashboard converts raw data into meaningful insights with user-friendly filters and visuals.

Tools & Skills Used
•	Microsoft Excel
•	Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
•	Slicers & Interactive Filters
•	Data Cleaning & Preparation

Key Features
•	KPI cards: Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Quantity, Profit Margin
•	Year-wise and Month-wise trend charts
•	Top products and top cities analysis
•	Category & sub-category breakdown
•	Region-wise performance comparison
•	Fully interactive filters (Year, Month, State)

Impact / Results
•	Improved decision-making with clear and actionable business insights
•	Quick identification of high-performing products and regions
•	Easy tracking of business trends and performance patterns
•	Enhanced visibility into sales and profit data
